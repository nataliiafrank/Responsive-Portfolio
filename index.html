About Me

My name is Nataliia but everybody calls me Natasha. I grew up in the beautiful city of Kiev in Ukraine. Recently I moved to the United States to live with my husband in his home country.

I have a bachelors degree in psychology, I had a lot of fun studying it in college. I love learning everything about the human brain and body, about productivity, self-improvement and the learning process itself.

I’m a person of broad interests, I’ve tried and enjoyed doing a lot of things including photography, making my own clothes, ballet dancing, professional hiking and even hairdressing. But my current passion is computers. I’ve always liked constant learning and a good challenge. I love the web world and I couldn’t imagine my life without it (just like everybody else nowadays). My goal is to be able to make beautiful and intuitive web sites and applications and make the Internet a nice friendly virtual environment.

I also love learning English and I keep improving my language skills every day.
